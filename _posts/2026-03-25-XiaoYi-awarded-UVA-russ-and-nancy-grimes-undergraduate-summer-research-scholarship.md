XiaoYi Luo, an undergraduate researcher in the Yang Lab, has been awarded the UVA Russ and Nancy Grimes Undergraduate Summer Research Scholarship. This award provides a $5,000 scholarship to support outstanding undergraduate students pursuing independent summer research.

Congratulations to XiaoYi!

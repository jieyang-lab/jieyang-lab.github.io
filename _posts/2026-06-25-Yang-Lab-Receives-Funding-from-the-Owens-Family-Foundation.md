excerpt: "The Yang Lab has been awarded a three-year, $300,000 philanthropic research grant from the Owens Family Foundation to support studies of mitochondrial stress signaling."
---
We are excited to share that the Yang Lab has received philanthropic support from the Owens Family Foundation to advance our research on mitochondrial stress response and integrated stress respons signaling. This generous support will enable us to pursue innovative, high-risk research aimed at uncovering the molecular mechanisms of mitochondrial stress signaling and developing novel therapeutic strategies for human disease. Philanthropic investments like this provide critical flexibility to explore bold scientific ideas and accelerate discoveries that may ultimately benefit patients. We are deeply grateful to the Owens Family Foundation for their confidence in our research and our vision. We look forward to sharing our progress in the years ahead.
